🏁 Top 3 Go-Kart Tracks

  1. K1 Speed (Nationwide)
    • Electric indoor racing with speeds up to 45 mph
    • Locations across the U.S., including Salt Lake City
  2. Trackhouse Motorplex (Mooresville, NC)
    • Outdoor, professional-style track used by NASCAR pros
    • 0.7-mile track with 11 turns
  3. Las Vegas Mini Grand Prix (Las Vegas, NV)
    • Multiple outdoor tracks including sprint and kiddie karts
    • Perfect mix of competition and family fun
